#Slide: 3
#Header: Identifying Bad Friends
#Content: 
- Bad friends can be identified through their negative behavior and influence.
- They may exhibit traits such as manipulation, dishonesty, and lack of support.
- It is important to recognize these signs to protect ourselves from toxic friendships.

#Slide: 4
#Header: Impact of Bad Friends
#Content: 
- Bad friends can have a detrimental impact on our mental and emotional well-being.
- They may encourage negative behaviors, such as substance abuse or risky activities.
- Their influence can lead to a decline in self-esteem and overall happiness.

#Slide: 5
#Header: Signs of a Toxic Friendship
#Content: 
- Signs of a toxic friendship include constant criticism, jealousy, and lack of trust.
- They may prioritize their own needs and disregard your feelings.
- It is crucial to be aware of these signs to maintain healthy relationships.

#Slide: 6
#Header: Dealing with Bad Friends
#Content: 
- When dealing with bad friends, it is important to set boundaries and communicate openly.
- Consider distancing yourself from toxic individuals and seeking support from other friends or professionals.
- Remember that your well-being should always be a priority.

#Slide: 7
#Header: Conclusion
#Content: 
- In conclusion, creating a PowerPoint presentation about bad friends allows us to raise awareness about the impact of toxic friendships.
- By identifying the signs and learning how to deal with bad friends, we can cultivate healthier relationships and prioritize our own well-being.
